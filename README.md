## The Five Kingdoms

Each kingdom was founded on one aspect of humanity — and has been starved of the other four.

| Kingdom | Aspect | One line |
|---------|--------|----------|
| **[Luminar](world/kingdoms/luminar.md)** | Creativity | Boundless invention and art, with no purpose or ethics to guide it |
| **[Veritas](world/kingdoms/veritas.md)** | Science | Deep understanding of the natural world, with no wonder or community to share it |
| **[Forgeholm](world/kingdoms/forgeholm.md)** | Technology | Powerful tools and industry, with no wisdom to ask whether they should |
| **[Sophara](world/kingdoms/sophara.md)** | Philosophy | Wisdom and deep inquiry, paralyzed — thought without action |
| **[Agoran](world/kingdoms/agoran.md)** | Community | Belonging with nothing to hold it together — **fallen to the king** |

Full detail: [World Overview](world/overview.md)
