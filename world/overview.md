# World Overview

## The Five Kingdoms

Each kingdom was founded on one of five essential aspects of humanity. For generations they coexisted, their differences complementing one another. Over time — through pride, conflict, or circumstance — they withdrew into themselves. Now each kingdom has mastered its aspect while being starved of the others, and the cracks in that isolation are beginning to show.

| Kingdom | Aspect | What they have | What they lack |
|---------|--------|----------------|----------------|
| **Luminar** | Creativity | Boundless invention, art, expression | Purpose, ethics — creation for its own sake |
| **Veritas** | Science | Deep understanding of the natural world | Wonder, meaning, community to share discovery |
| **Forgeholm** | Technology | Powerful tools, engineering, industry | Wisdom to guide it, humanity in its application |
| **Sophara** | Philosophy | Wisdom, ethics, deep inquiry | Action — thought without application becomes paralysis |
| **Agoran** | Community | Belonging, solidarity, shared labor | Tradition, song, meaning — togetherness with no purpose |

## The World at a Glance

**Central tension:** The five kingdoms have fallen into disrepair through isolation. Each has excelled in its aspect, but been deprived of the others — and that imbalance has left them fractured and vulnerable. An evil king has risen and taken control of the Kingdom of Community, and is now moving on Technology. The realm needs heroes who can bridge the divides between kingdoms and unite them before he claims them all.

**The evil king's strategy:** He targets kingdoms weakened by isolation. Community, hollowed out by its lack of creativity and philosophy, was ripe — people desperate for purpose and meaning are easy to manipulate with promises of belonging. Technology is next: powerful tools in the hands of someone without wisdom or ethics.

**The heroes' task:** Not just to defeat a king, but to help five kingdoms remember what they need from each other.
